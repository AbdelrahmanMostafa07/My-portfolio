Sales Management Project






Business Request:


The business request for this Sales Project was an executive sales report for sales managers, Based on the request that was made from the business we following user stories were defined to fulfill delivery and ensure that acceptance criteria’s were maintained throughout the project


Sales Manager Dashboard Overview:

Objective: Obtain a dashboard overview of internet sales to track top-performing customers and products.


Sales Representative Customer Overview:

Objective: Access a detailed Internet Sales overview per customer for effective follow-ups and identifying sales opportunities.


Sales Representative Product Overview:

Objective: Access detailed Internet Sales information per product to monitor top-performing products.


Sales Manager Sales Overview Against Budget:

Objective: Obtain a dashboard overview of internet sales to track sales over time against the budget.
